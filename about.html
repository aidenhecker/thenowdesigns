Why we exist

We got tired of template factories.

Most websites are sold like furniture: pick a template, swap the photos, ship it. Then it loads slow, looks like everyone else, and quietly costs you customers.

We started The Now Designs to do the opposite. Every site is hand-built — designed around your brand and your buyers, coded to load before they blink, and written to actually convert. No drag-and-drop bloat, no "good enough."

The name's the whole philosophy: design for the now. The web your customers use today — fast, mobile-first, beautiful, and honest. Not a 2015 theme with your logo dropped on top.

And because we keep the roster small, you don't get handed off to a junior or buried in a ticket queue. You talk to the person building it, you see real progress every week, and you own everything when it's done.
