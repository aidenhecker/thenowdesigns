# The Now Designs — Pricing & Path to First \$10K

> Bands are bands, not quotes — the real number is set on the call against the client's value.

## Pricing reconciliation

RECONCILE — one KEEP, one RESTRUCTURE+RAISE, one KEEP-with-a-gate, plus one ADD. Provenance: Bible §10 gives a solo-dev band of $1.5K–8K for accent/storytelling craft; §14 says price to client VALUE, not your time (5-page oil firm ≈ $15K, same pages for a cupcake shop ≈ $500). Your live $1,999 custom tier sits at the absolute FLOOR of §10 — you are charging like a freelancer while positioning like a studio. That mismatch is the single biggest leak.

TIER 1 — "Built & Maintained" ($99/mo + $900 start): KEEP. This is a smart productized on-ramp and your durable MRR seed — do not touch the monthly through Phase 1; the recurring is the whole point. The only soft spot is the $900 start being thin for the real build effort. Phase 1 (first 1–2 deals, buying logos): leave it at $900. Phase 2 (after proof): raise the start to $1,500 and new clients to $129/mo (grandfather existing clients at $99 — that loyalty story sells). Reasoning: a subscription site is a retention bet; the start fee should at least cover your first month of build bandwidth.

TIER 2 — "Full Custom" ($1,999 one-time, "Buy now" checkout): RESTRUCTURE + RAISE — highest-leverage change on the whole site. (1) Kill the direct-to-checkout "Buy now" for this tier — a $1,999 click-to-pay caps your price at the published number, skips the §14 proposal where value is actually captured, and (per the copy audit) produces abandoned carts, not signed projects. Change the CTA to "Book a call first." Keep one tiny secondary "already decided? skip to checkout" link for pre-sold buyers only. (2) Convert the fixed price into a SCOPED BAND sold via proposal with 50% deposit. New numbers: Phase 1 FOUNDING rate $2,500–3,000 for your first 2–3 clients, explicitly traded for a testimonial + case-study rights + one referral intro (frame it as a founding-client exchange, never a discount-from-desperation — Bible §14 scarcity-from-abundance). Phase 2 STANDARD band $4,500–6,000; PREMIUM $7,500–12,000 for high-LTV businesses (clinic, law firm, trades, anyone whose single new customer is worth four figures — that is the oil-firm-vs-cupcake test in action). Reasoning: even your founding $3,000 doubles take-per-deal vs $1,999 (3 sales to $10K instead of 5), and a $1,999 "fully custom, you own everything" headline actively undercuts award-mode credibility — price IS a quality signal.

ADD-ON — "SEO & Advertising" ($500/mo): KEEP, but GATE it. Attach it at handover, not at the sale, and set a 90-day expectation up front ("give it three months — SEO compounds, it doesn't spike"). Reasoning: $500/mo is your MRR multiplier and the most corroborated lever you own; the only failure mode is selling it month-to-month and letting a client judge it after 30 days.

ADD A FOURTH OFFER — "Care Plan" $150–250/mo: your $50/mo hosting on the one-time tier is near pass-through and leaves every Full-Custom client as a one-and-done. Bundle hosting + monthly content edits + uptime/perf monitoring + priority support into a Care Plan and offer it at handover. Reasoning: it converts one-time buyers into recurring revenue — the difference between a project business and a compounding one — and it is the cheapest MRR you will ever add because the relationship already exists.

NET: KEEP the $99/mo on-ramp, RESTRUCTURE the custom tier off checkout into a $2.5–3K founding / $4.5–6K standard scoped band, KEEP+gate the $500/mo SEO, and ADD a $150–250/mo Care Plan. Hold all bands as bands, not quotes — the real number is set on the call against the client's value.

## Recommended path — PATH C — '3 Founding Builds + 1 Retainer' (cash + proof + MRR seed, in ~8–10 weeks). This is the recommended path because the first $10K's real job is not the cash — it's buying the PROOF that unlocks the price raise. Weight it toward custom builds because each build = one case study + one testimonial.

**The math:**

Using their REAL prices with the Phase-1 founding restructure ($3,000/custom, sold 50/50): 3 founding Full-Custom builds x $3,000 = $9,000 (each lands $1,500 deposit at signing + $1,500 on launch). Attach the SEO & Advertising add-on to ONE of those clients at handover: $500/mo x 2 months by the third launch = $1,000. TOTAL = $10,000, hit on the third build's launch. You exit with 3 shipped case studies, 3 named testimonials, and $500/mo recurring already running — which is exactly the proof needed to move the custom tier from $3,000 founding to the $4,500–6,000 standard band. Contrast at the CURRENT price: $1,999 x 5 = $9,995 — same $10K, but 5 sales and 5 builds instead of 3-4. Raising to $3,000 is literally 'two fewer builds for the same money.'

## Alternative paths

- PATH A — 'Custom-led, fastest cash, thin engine': At the current $1,999, you need 5 one-time builds (5 x $1,999 = $9,995) — five separate sales cycles, no MRR left behind, and you've sold five custom sites for the price studios charge for two. Fast if your pipeline is hot, but it leaves you back at zero with no recurring. Use ONLY if you need cash this month and can't hold the founding-rate line yet. The mentor note: even here, raise to $2,500 founding and you hit $10K in 4 builds, not 5.

- PATH B — 'MRR stack, durable but slow to first cash': Fill the roster you already advertise (6 clients at a time). 6 Built-&-Maintained subs = 6 x $900 start ($5,400 one-time) + 6 x $99/mo ($594/mo) + 2 SEO add-ons x $500/mo ($1,000/mo). First-quarter cash = $5,400 starts + ($594 + $1,000) x 3 = $5,400 + $4,782 = $10,182 — crosses $10K late in month 3 and then throws off $1,594/mo forever. Best long-term economics, but it's 8 client relationships for a solo and the slowest route to the first dollar of proof. Graduate INTO this from Path C once you have testimonials to close subs faster.
