Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how The Now Designs ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit thenowdesigns.com (the "Site") or work with us.

Information we collect

Information you give us. When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the details of your message. When you use the free website scanner, the public website address you enter is sent to our diagnostic provider so that page can be loaded and tested. If you choose to generate an AI concept, the public page title, description, hostname, scan scores, and technical findings are sent through our protected service to our AI provider. Contact-form names and email addresses are not included in that request. Do not submit private, password-protected, or internal addresses. When you request payment approval or purchase a plan, you provide billing details directly to our payment processor (see below).

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, basic technical data (such as your browser type and pages viewed) may be processed by our hosting and third-party providers. We do not run our own advertising or cross-site tracking.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide our services;
  • To process payments and manage your subscription or project;
  • To improve the Site and our services;
  • To send service-related communications you've requested.

Payments

Payments are processed by Whop and its payment partners. We do not store or have access to your full card details. Please review Whop's privacy practices for how they handle your payment information.

Third-party services

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to operate the Site:

  • GitHub Pages — website hosting;
  • Microlink — loading the public URL you choose and returning page metadata, a screenshot, and automated Lighthouse diagnostics;
  • Cloudflare Workers — protecting and operating the optional AI concept service;
  • OpenRouter — generating the optional structured website concept from the public scan summary;
  • Web3Forms — delivering contact-form submissions to our inbox;
  • Whop — checkout and payment processing;
  • Google Fonts — typography delivery.

Each provider processes data under its own privacy policy.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies and similar storage needed to remember your cookie choice and operate the Site. See our Cookie Policy for details.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with the service providers listed above as needed to run the Site and our services, or where required by law.

Data retention

We keep contact and project information for as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal obligations, then delete or anonymise it. Generated concept results may be cached for up to seven days to avoid repeating paid processing for the same scan.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or object to its processing. To make a request, email us at aiden@thenowdesigns.com.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures (including HTTPS encryption) to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page, and we'll update the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email aiden@thenowdesigns.com.
