Security · not optional

It's not about the website. It's about keeping what's yours.

AI didn't just change how websites get built — it changed how they get attacked. Bots, phishing nets, and scrapers run around the clock, below the surface, headless — never even opening a browser. They harvest data, clone ideas, and hijack identities before anyone notices. A website is the easy part. Keeping you safe online is the real work.

⚠ The threat is invisible

Most attacks never touch a browser. Automated bots probe, scrape, and phish below the surface, 24/7. If a site isn't locked down at the wire, you won't see it happen — you'll only see the damage.

💡 Your ideas are the target

In an AI world, your unique ideas are what make you money. The wrong setup lets anyone copy your content, lift your data, and profit off what you built. We make sure your ideas keep paying you.

Good questions

Before you ask.

How long does a website take?

Most builds go live in 4–6 weeks, not 4–6 months. Bigger or custom projects can take longer — we'll give you a real timeline on the first call, and weekly progress so you're never wondering.

Do I own the website when it's done?

Completely. You own the code, the domain, and the data — forever. No vendor lock-in. If you ever leave, you walk away with everything.

Do you build on Squarespace, WordPress, or custom code?

Whatever serves you best. We hand-code custom sites for maximum speed and control, but we'll work in your platform if that's the right call. The goal is your result, not our preference.

What if I just need a redesign, not a full build?

That's fine. We take on redesigns, single pages, custom sections, and marketing-only engagements. Tell us where you are on the call and we'll scope exactly what you need.

Will my site actually rank on Google?

SEO architecture is built into every site from line one, and speed (which Google rewards) is a given. For ongoing rankings, our SEO & Advertising add-on ($500/mo) handles local SEO, content, and reporting month to month.

Can I pay over time?
