Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how The Now Designs uses cookies and similar storage technologies on thenowdesigns.com.

What are cookies?

Cookies (and similar technologies like local storage) are small data files stored on your device. They help websites remember your choices and function properly.

How we use them

We keep our use minimal. We do not run advertising or cross-site tracking.

  • Essential storage. We store your cookie-consent choice locally on your device so we don't ask you again on every visit.
  • Form functionality. When you submit the contact form, our form provider may set temporary data needed to process and protect against spam.

Third-party cookies

Some third parties we use may set their own cookies when their features load:

  • Whop — when you proceed to checkout, Whop and its payment partners may set cookies to process your purchase securely.
  • Google Fonts — used to deliver typography; it does not set advertising cookies.

These are governed by each provider's own cookie and privacy policies.

Managing cookies

You can accept or decline non-essential cookies via the banner shown on your first visit. You can also control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how parts of the Site work.

Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
