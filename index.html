Our method · neuro-informed design

We design for the brain — before the eye.

Attention is decided in the first five seconds, below conscious thought. We engineer every page around how the brain actually reads, feels, and decides — then refine it against attention and conversion research before it ships.

⚡ The 5-second window

The brain reads the emotional register of a page before the eyes finish their first pass. We design the first frame for that fast emotional path.

◆ Anticipation, then payoff

We build pages with deliberate tension: a question the headline opens, then the resolution that earns the scroll.

◇ A review before it ships

Before anything leaves our hands, we run every page through a structured design review built around cognitive science, conversion research, and attention principles. Visual hierarchy, emotional pacing, friction points, and the single most important action — checked against each other until the page earns the click.
