Good questions

Before you ask.

How long does a website take?

Most builds go live in 4–6 weeks, not 4–6 months. Bigger or custom projects can take longer — we'll give you a real timeline on the first call, and weekly progress so you're never wondering.

Do I own the website when it's done?

Completely. You own the code, the domain, and the data — forever. No vendor lock-in. If you ever leave, you walk away with everything.

Do you build on Squarespace, WordPress, or custom code?

Whatever serves you best. We hand-code custom sites for maximum speed and control, but we'll work in your platform if that's the right call. The goal is your result, not our preference.

What if I just need a redesign, not a full build?

That's fine. We take on redesigns, single pages, custom sections, and marketing-only engagements. Tell us where you are on the call and we'll scope exactly what you need.

Will my site actually rank on Google?

SEO architecture is built into every site from line one, and speed (which Google rewards) is a given. For ongoing rankings, our Growth service handles local SEO, content, and reporting month to month.

Is financing available?

Yes — every build can be financed over 6, 12, or 24 months. We'll lay out the options on the call so you can start now and spread the cost.
